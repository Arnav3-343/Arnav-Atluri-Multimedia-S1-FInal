Bio

My name is Arnav Atluri, and I am a Multimedia student at Evergreen Valley High School. This semester, I explored digital art and design while learning how to apply the Elements of Art and Principles of Design to create stronger and more intentional compositions. I enjoy creating work that uses strong contrast, clear focal points, and thoughtful color choices to communicate ideas effectively. For this portfolio, I chose a Yu-Gi-Oh and Egyptian tomb theme to present my artwork as artifacts in a gallery, which reflects my interest in combining visual storytelling with design concepts.
