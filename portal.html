Portal Map

Click a relic to open a chamber (page).

This Portal Map functions as a visual navigation system for the portfolio. Inspired by the Exodia gallery layout, each framed section acts as a clickable relic that leads to a different chamber of the site. This design emphasizes unity and movement while keeping navigation engaging and thematic.
