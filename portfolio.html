Required: 3–5 artworks with medium + why you chose it.

Composition Study (Principles of Design)

Medium: Digital design ContrastPlacementScale

I chose this piece because it clearly demonstrates my understanding of composition and visual hierarchy. I used contrast and placement to create a strong focal point and guide the viewer’s eye through the design. The scale of the elements helps establish emphasis while maintaining balance across the composition.

Collage / Poster Design

Medium: Digital collage EmphasisValueBalance

I selected this artwork because it shows my ability to combine multiple visual elements into a unified design. I focused on balance and value to make sure the main subject stands out while the background supports it. This piece helped me practice emphasis and organization without making the composition feel cluttered.

Speed of Light Poster

Medium: Vector illustration ColorRhythmMovement

I chose this poster because it strongly represents my use of color, rhythm, and movement. The repeated running figures create a sense of motion, while the bold color choices add energy to the design. The central lightning symbol creates emphasis and ties the composition together visually.
