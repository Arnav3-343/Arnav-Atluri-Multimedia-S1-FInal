Multimedia Reflection

Required: what multimedia is, what you learned, what you want to learn (your own words).

Multimedia is the combination of visual art, design, and technology to communicate ideas in creative and effective ways. Throughout this semester, I learned how the Elements of Art—such as line, shape, color, value, texture, and space—and the Principles of Design—such as contrast, balance, emphasis, unity, movement, and rhythm—can significantly improve the clarity and impact of a design. By applying these concepts, I was able to create compositions with stronger focal points and better visual hierarchy. Next semester, I want to improve my skills in motion graphics, animation, and typography so that my work feels more dynamic, professional, and polished.
